About

Welcome to Indie Swaad! Here, our culinary journey began with a pinch of nostalgia and a dash of passion. Our chefs—artists in aprons—craft dishes that tell stories. From farm-fresh ingredients to secret family recipes, every plate is a canvas. We’re not just a restaurant; we’re a neighborhood gathering spot. Our cozy corners echo laughter, and our rooftop garden whispers secrets. Whether you’re savoring our signature pasta or sipping a handcrafted cocktail, you’re part of our culinary family. Join us. Taste the love. Cheers to good food and great company!
